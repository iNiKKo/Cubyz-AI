## Summary
The issue introduces a new feature called 'Tree Tapping' which allows players to extract sap and resin from certain logs. The mechanic includes crafting tappers, placing them on sappy logs, collecting resin in buckets, and processing it into usable items.

## Explanation
The Tree Tapping feature is designed as a renewable resource system for obtaining materials like sap, resin, syrup, and amber. It introduces new block types (sappy logs) and items (tappers, raw resin bucket). The mechanic aims to provide a farming-like experience in the game. Reviewers suggest adding durability to tappers and depleting sappy logs after a certain amount of tapping to prevent infinite resource generation. They also discuss how to inform players about this new feature through natural spawning mechanics.
