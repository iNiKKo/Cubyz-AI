## Summary
The issue discusses adding various plants and materials to different biomes within the game Cubyz.

## Explanation
This review discusses enhancing the game's environment by introducing new flora elements such as Castilleja, Daisies, Ferns, Ivy, Autumn leaves, Dead leaves, leaf piles, and Clay into various biomes. Specifically:
- **Castilleja** should generate in deserts rarely.
- **Daisies** could be added to any grassy biome.
- **Ferns** should appear more frequently in cold biomes compared to other biomes.
- **Ivy** should appear as ground cover in biomes with less grass, especially in jungles.
- **Autumn leaves**, **Dead leaves**, and **leaf piles** need their biomes defined for inclusion.
- **Clay** could look nice in some dry biomes. The maintainer confirms that all these additions have been implemented.
