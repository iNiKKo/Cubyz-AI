## Summary
The discussion revolves around balancing tool damage and durability in Cubyz. Maintainers propose various solutions including non-linear damage reduction, randomization of swing times, and making swing times constant based on player strength.

## Explanation
The issue centers around the balance between tool damage and durability when mining blocks. The main concern is that tools with slightly suboptimal swings become significantly less efficient, doubling their swing time and halving their durability. Maintainers explore several solutions: non-linear damage reduction to lessen the penalty for being close to required damage; randomizing swing times to introduce variability without changing the average time; and making swing times constant based on player strength to simplify tool usage. The discussion also touches on the importance of transparency in mechanics, avoiding 'invisible mechanics' that frustrate players, and ensuring tools remain intuitive and understandable.
