## Summary
The proposal discusses adding a sapphire gemstone with specific physical and in-game properties, including an 'energy-steal' modifier. There is discussion about the appropriateness of assigning certain modifiers to gems based on their fictional counterparts.

## Explanation
The proposal discusses adding a sapphire gemstone with specific physical and in-game properties, including an 'energy-steal' modifier. The physical properties of sapphire are as follows: hardness >=9 on the Mohs scale and density of 4 g/cm3 at 20°C. In-game, it behaves like other gemstones but provides special effects instead of stats. The main point of contention is the assignment of the 'energy-steal' modifier. Initially proposed for sapphire, there are concerns about its alignment with the fictional counterpart of ruby (which has 'life-steal'). The maintainer suggests moving the 'energy-steal' modifier to another gem like Amethyst instead. There is also a suggestion to introduce a new concept, such as a soul-slurping creature's leather having a 'soul-steal' effect. Sapphire spawns in the range -1700 < Z < -7000 and never spawns in veins.
