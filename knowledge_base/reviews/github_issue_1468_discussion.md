## Summary
The discussion revolves around designing totems as magical blocks that enhance gameplay immersion, including features like respawn points, protection, and various effects. The maintainers propose a multi-block structure with different types of totem components, each requiring special materials for crafting.

## Explanation
The maintainers suggest adding a dedicated totem top block as a power source, ensuring that the remaining totem functions only when this block is present. They also propose that totems should not drop anything when destroyed and should be crafted using magical wood and rare items. The respawn mechanic would involve a timer that increases with distance from the totem, providing an advantage to base defenders. Additionally, they explore ideas for accessories and items that work with totems, such as tools with increased durability within a certain range of the totem. The discussion also touches on potential cultural concerns regarding the representation of indigenous practices and suggests alternative designs like tree-like structures or standing stones.
