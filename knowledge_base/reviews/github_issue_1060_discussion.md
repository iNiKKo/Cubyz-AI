## Summary
Discussion on adding different types of storage blocks (Chest, Crate, Barrel) with varying capacities and designs.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around the introduction of new storage blocks in Cubyz. The suggestions include a standard Chest with multiple slots, a Crate that holds a total number of items without individual slots, and a Barrel for bulk storage of one type of item. There is debate about the capacity of these chests and whether they should occupy full blocks or be slightly smaller like in Minecraft. The maintainer suggests balancing the design to encourage players to build larger houses or storage structures over time.
