## Explanation
Discussion on adding different types of storage blocks (Chest, Crate, Barrel) with varying capacities and designs. The Chest is a standard chest with slots for items; it has 9 slots where each slot can hold up to 240 items. The Crate holds a total number of items rather than individual slots, making it ideal for storing various items in small quantities. The Barrel holds a very large amount of one item but only stores one type and occupies a full block of space. There is debate about the capacity of these chests and whether they should occupy full blocks or be slightly smaller like in Minecraft. The maintainer suggests balancing the design to encourage players to build larger houses or storage structures over time, with each chest occupying 1 block of space.
