## Explanation
Discussion about changing the tool required to mine mushrooms from an axe to a sickle, with considerations for the effectiveness of sickles on full mushroom blocks. The maintainers suggest that while sickles should be effective on individual mushrooms, they should have half the damage dealt when used on full mushroom blocks due to their bulkier nature. This decision is based on the specialization of tools: sickles are more specialized for cutting small plants like single mushrooms, whereas axes are better suited for thicker materials such as full mushroom blocks. There's also a mention of potential future changes related to giant mushrooms in issue #1678, which could affect how these tools interact with larger mushroom structures.
