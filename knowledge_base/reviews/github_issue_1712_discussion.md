## Summary
Discussion about changing the tool required to mine mushrooms from an axe to a sickle, with considerations for the effectiveness of sickles on full mushroom blocks.

## Explanation
The issue revolves around modifying the game mechanics to allow single mushrooms to be mined using a sickle instead of an axe. The maintainers suggest that while sickles should be effective on individual mushrooms, they should be less effective on full mushroom blocks due to their bulkier nature. This decision is based on the specialization of tools: sickles for small plants and axes for thicker materials. There's also a mention of potential future changes related to giant mushrooms in issue #1678, which could affect how these tools interact with larger mushroom structures.
