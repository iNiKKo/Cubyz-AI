## Summary
Discussion about adding normal mapping and reflectivity maps to enhance crystal and obsidian textures in Cubyz.

## Explanation
Discussion about adding normal mapping for rough reflective substances like crystal and obsidian to enhance their textures in Cubyz. The maintainer attempted this but encountered issues with compatibility with the current fake reflection effect, which does not play well with normal mapping. Additionally, the implementation is deemed expensive in terms of performance. Reflectivity maps have been added for composite blocks.
