## Summary
Discussion about implementing various types of locks for blocks in Cubyz, including material-based keys, tool-based locks, transparency locks, and high-tier locks. Maintainer notes that chest locks were previously rejected due to planned protector blocks.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around the implementation of different lock mechanisms in Cubyz, such as using specific materials or tools as keys, allowing interaction with blocks behind locked ones, and preventing chests from spilling their contents without the correct key. The maintainer points out that chest locks were previously rejected due to the planned introduction of protector blocks, which serve a similar purpose but are based on knowledge rather than physical keys. Users suggest that locks could still be useful in servers where protector blocks are disabled or as an alternative solution for certain structures.
