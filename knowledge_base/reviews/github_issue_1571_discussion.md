## Summary
The discussion revolves around implementing a 'strength' mechanic for tools in Cubyz, where tool usage depends on player strength relative to the tool's mass. Maintainers suggest alternatives like stamina or energy consumption without an explicit strength stat.

## Explanation
The proposal introduces a new 'strength' attribute for players that affects their ability to use tools based on the tool's mass. The main points of contention include whether to add a visible 'strength' stat, how to stack and wear off strength effects, and the impact on inventory space with permanent accessories. Maintainers propose alternatives such as stamina or energy consumption per swing, aiming to avoid an explicit strength stat to prevent complexity and potential frustration for players.
