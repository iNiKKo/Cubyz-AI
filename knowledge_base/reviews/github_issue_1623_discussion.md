## Explanation
Discussion about reducing stone tool durability to encourage progression. The maintainers suggest simply decreasing the durability rather than using a modifier that affects all tools. There is debate over whether stone tools are problematic due to their ease of creation and lack of downsides compared to copper tools, which are slower but deal more damage and last longer. Specifically, copper tools have 3× less swing time, higher damage output, and better durability compared to stone tools. The maintainers argue that with current balancing, copper tools offer better value in terms of speed, durability, and damage output.
