## Explanation
Discussion on implementing thin ice in the game, focusing on how to handle fall damage translation to block damage. Specifically, thin ice forms on the surface of water in freezing biomes and melts when exposed to heat sources. Regular ice, crafted from thin ice, cannot be melted and is used for building purposes. The discussion includes an idea where fall damage you take can directly translate to block damage, making thin ice break more easily due to its brittleness. However, this implementation might cause lag, and further development would require issue #2938.
