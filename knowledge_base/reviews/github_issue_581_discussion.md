## Summary
Discussion on adding downwards momentum to player movement in Cubyz.

## Explanation
The issue revolves around enhancing the player's walking and sprinting mechanics by introducing downwards momentum. The primary concern is ensuring that this momentum is only applied when the player is grounded, to prevent unintended flying off stairs. There are suggestions for alternative approaches such as reducing momentum when descending without jumping or implementing a step-down mechanism that increases downward velocity instead of teleporting.
