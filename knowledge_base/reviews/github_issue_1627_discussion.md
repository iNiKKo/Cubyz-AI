## Explanation
The discussion centers around adding a modifier to tools that increases their chance of breaking with each successful swing when at zero durability, starting from a 1% chance and increasing by 1% for every subsequent swing. The maintainers acknowledge the balancing difficulty but are enthusiastic about enhancing gameplay through such modifiers. Additionally, there is an optional critical damage aspect where using the tool at this point deals increased damage, adding a risk/reward element to gameplay.
