## Summary
The issue describes a bug where blocks behind an instantly mined block are damaged due to excessive tool damage.

## Explanation
The reviewer points out that the observed behavior is not actually caused by insta-mining or undertime, but rather by the tool's damage being too high compared to the block health. This indicates a balancing issue with the tool's attributes rather than a bug in the mining mechanics.
