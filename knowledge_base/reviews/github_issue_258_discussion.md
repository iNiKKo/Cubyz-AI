## Summary
The discussion revolves around adding stat boosts for sleeping well, with concerns about keeping core mechanics simple and avoiding excessive additional requirements.

## Explanation
Sleeping well in Cubyz could boost player statistics such as movement speed and stamina regeneration. The maintainers are concerned about keeping core mechanics simple and avoiding excessive additional requirements. They suggest focusing on enhancing sleep quality through factors like environment conditions (e.g., windows, sky visibility, decoration blocks, a ventilated fireplace) and equipment worn during sleep (e.g., whether the player sleeps with their armor on). One maintainer also mentions the possibility of simulating certain gases (e.g., radioactive fog) that could damage players. However, this is a separate consideration from the main discussion about stat boosts.
