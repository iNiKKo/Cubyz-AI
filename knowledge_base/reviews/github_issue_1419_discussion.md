## Explanation
The discussion revolves around implementing different tiers of torches in Cubyz, inspired by Minecraft's lighting mechanics. The proposal includes wood torches for basic exploration, which are a lot dimmer than standard coal torches. Coal torches provide brighter light and can be used to light campfires for free. Bright white glowcrystal torches are suggested as the highest tier, offering maximum brightness similar to white glowcrystals. Additionally, there is a suggestion for sticky torches that can be thrown and automatically attach to surfaces, which could be useful for measuring depth in caves. The discussion also touches on the possibility of making wooden torches burn out over time but notes that this feature would be trivial with #1338. However, it is noted that buildings should not stop functioning due to burning-out torches, and manual extinguishing might be allowed in certain structures for a unique gameplay experience. The introduction of cloth as an alternative material for crafting wooden torches is also mentioned, suggesting planks+cloth as a better recipe than just planks+wood. There is also a suggestion to include coal in above-ground boulders to fix exploration issues by making it less accessible near cave entrances.
