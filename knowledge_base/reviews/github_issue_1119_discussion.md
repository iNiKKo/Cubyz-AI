## Summary
The discussion revolves around how to handle multiple modifiers of the same type on tools in Cubyz. The maintainers suggest a diminishing returns system based on the location of the modifier within the tool's crafting grid.

## Explanation
The issue explores two primary options for handling duplicate modifiers: adding them together or ignoring subsequent instances. The maintainers lean towards a diminishing returns approach, where the effectiveness of a modifier decreases based on its position in the tool's crafting grid. This encourages strategic placement of modifiers rather than stacking duplicates. The discussion also touches on alternative ideas like runes and socketing systems from other games, but these are ultimately dismissed in favor of simplicity and maintaining procedural crafting depth.
