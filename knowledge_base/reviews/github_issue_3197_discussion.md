## Summary
The discussion revolves around a proposed feature to simplify stone management in Cubyz by consolidating stone variants into a universal stone item. The feature aims to reduce inventory clutter while maintaining functionality.

## Explanation
The proposal suggests that when stone blocks are broken, they drop a universal stone item instead of their specific variant. This would allow players to choose the desired stone variant from a menu while holding the stone item. The maintainers discuss various aspects of this feature, including user experience, game balance, and potential impacts on exploration. They explore alternative solutions such as letting the hotbar take over for block selection and consider the implications of making stone transmutable into any type. The maintainers also address concerns about making the process too easy and losing the essence of gameplay.
