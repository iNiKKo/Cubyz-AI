## Explanation
The discussion revolves around a proposed feature to simplify stone management in Cubyz by consolidating stone variants into a universal stone item. When stone blocks are broken, they drop a universal stone item instead of their specific variant (e.g., Slate Bricks, Slate Cobblestone, and Smooth Slate all drop into the same thing). This allows players to choose the desired stone variant from a menu while holding the stone item. The maintainers discuss various aspects of this feature, including user experience, game balance, and potential impacts on exploration.

Alternative solutions explored include letting the hotbar take over for block selection (e.g., pressing middle mouse button to toggle between specific slate types in the hotbar). There is also a concern about making stone transmutable into any type, which could remove the need to visit different biomes to collect different materials. The maintainers address concerns about making the process too easy, potentially cutting out a chunk of the non-procedural crafting system, and losing the essence of gameplay. They suggest that the feature should be exclusive to stones and woods (if woods ever get a decorated variant) and consider leaving this idea on the backburner for now.
