## Explanation
Discussion about potential new names for Cubyz, focusing on criteria like uniqueness, memorability, and relevance to the game's identity. Users propose several names inspired by constructed languages such as Lojban (Kubli, Canpa, Larcu, Kunra, Calsle), Toaq (Cltoq, Baılea), and eberban (Tsau, Kelon). Each name has a specific meaning derived from its respective language. For example, Kubli means 'cube/regular polyhedron/polygon,' Canpa means 'shovel/spade for digging,' Larcu means 'art of craft/skill,' Kunra means 'mineral/ore of type/metal mined from location/lode/mine,' Calsle means 'voxel of model/object,' Cltoq means 'cube' in Toaq, Baılea means 'craft of,' Tsau means 'made/created/manufactured/crafted,' and Kelon means 'box.' The maintainers argue that Cubyz already fits well with these criteria, is easily searchable, has an established logo, and reflects the game's identity as a fast voxel engine base for modding. They conclude that renaming would not be worth the effort given Cubyz's current fit and recognition.
