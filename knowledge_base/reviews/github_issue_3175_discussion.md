## Summary

## Explanation
The discussion centers on enhancing Cubyz's environment through the introduction of rare natural landmarks such as broken thick trees, ruins, and rock formations. The maintainer suggests implementing rune blocks inspired by Wither 3's 'Places of Power,' which grant temporary effects to players upon interaction. These effects are intended to be similar to those available in-game but may offer unique or hard-to-obtain benefits. However, the maintainer also notes that making landmarks useful should be addressed separately from intrinsic design considerations, as not every structure needs extrinsic rewards.
