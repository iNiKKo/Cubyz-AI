## Summary
The maintainer expresses concern that isolated caves could encourage strip mining instead of exploring large cave systems.

## Explanation
The maintainer argues against the implementation of isolated caves because they believe it promotes a less engaging gameplay experience focused on strip mining rather than the exploration of expansive cave networks. The maintainer suggests that if there were an entrance to these isolated areas, they would be considered dungeons instead of caves, which might alter their intended use and purpose within the game.
