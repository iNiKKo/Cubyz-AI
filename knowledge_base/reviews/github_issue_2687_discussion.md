## Explanation
The issue revolves around modifying the visual representation of stars in the Cubyz game by replacing them with physical debris orbiting the world. The current approach uses a skybox, but there's a desire to replace it with dynamic, moving entities or projectiles that deal damage and explode upon impact with any block or entity due to their high speed. These debris are not blocks; they are specifically designed as entities or projectiles for gameplay mechanics. The discussion explores various ideas such as whether the stars should be replaced entirely or if they could exist in a different layer above the sky islands and debris layers, emphasizing that true stars would be far above these layers. There's also consideration for the thematic consistency of having 'fake' stars in a game where players build houses out of cubes.
