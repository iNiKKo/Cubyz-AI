## Summary
Discussion on replacing skybox stars with physical debris orbiting the Cubyz world, considering implications for gameplay and worldbuilding.

## Explanation
The issue revolves around modifying the visual representation of stars in the Cubyz game. The current approach uses a skybox, but there's a desire to replace it with dynamic, moving debris that orbits the world. This change introduces new gameplay mechanics where the debris can deal damage and explode upon impact. The discussion explores various ideas such as whether the stars should be replaced entirely or if they could exist in a different layer above the sky islands and debris layers. There's also consideration for the thematic consistency of having 'fake' stars in a game where players build houses out of cubes.
