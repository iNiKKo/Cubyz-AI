## Summary
Discussion about the overpowered nature of red mushrooms in Cubyz, suggesting changes to their bounce mechanics and fall damage absorption.

## Explanation
Discussion about the excessive bounciness of red mushrooms in Cubyz, which allows players to jump up to 20 blocks and easily return to ledges after falls. The maintainers discuss potential solutions such as making the mushrooms more trampoline-like, where players sink into them before bouncing back up. They also consider changing how fall damage is calculated, as red mushrooms currently do not absorb any energy from impacts, which affects fall damage determination. Specifically, they suggest implementing a mechanism similar to Mario Bros' spring boards and considering internal damage or bleeding to death after falls. The goal is to balance the game mechanics to prevent unintended advantages and ensure a fair gameplay experience.
