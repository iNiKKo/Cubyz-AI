## Summary
Discussion about the overpowered nature of red mushrooms in Cubyz, suggesting changes to their bounce mechanics and fall damage absorption.

## Explanation
The issue revolves around the excessive bounciness of red mushrooms, allowing players to jump extremely high and easily return to ledges after falls. The maintainers discuss potential solutions such as making the mushrooms more trampoline-like, where players sink into them before bouncing back up. They also consider changing how fall damage is calculated, as red mushrooms currently do not absorb any energy from impacts, which affects fall damage determination. The goal is to balance the game mechanics to prevent unintended advantages and ensure a fair gameplay experience.
