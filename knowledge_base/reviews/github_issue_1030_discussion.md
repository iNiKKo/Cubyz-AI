## Summary
The issue discusses the placement and behavior of vines in Cubyz, focusing on their ability to be placed horizontally and the need for different textures/models at the end of a vine chain.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around enhancing the vine mechanics in Cubyz. The primary concern is allowing vines to be placed horizontally while simulating the effect of dropping down a spool of rope. Additionally, there's a suggestion to introduce separate textures or models for the last block in a vine chain. The maintainer notes that making the entire vine break when the support block is destroyed is not feasible without implementing chained block updates, which could be complex.
