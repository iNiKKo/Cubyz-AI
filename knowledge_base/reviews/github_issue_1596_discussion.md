## Summary
The discussion revolves around making wood too resistant for players to break by hand, aiming to encourage the creation of axes. However, maintainers express concerns about player confusion and accessibility.

## Explanation
The proposal suggests making wood less common and harder to obtain by hand, requiring players to create tools like axes. This change aims to balance gameplay by incentivizing tool crafting and reducing reliance on easily accessible resources. Maintainers raise several concerns: the potential for player confusion due to similar-looking branches and logs, the impact on progression paths, and the accessibility of the game for new players coming from a Minecraft background. They argue that forcing players to differentiate between branches and logs might be too much of an artistic nuance and could lead to frustration if trees lack reachable branches.
