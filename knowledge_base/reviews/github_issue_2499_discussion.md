## Summary
Proposes adding an indicator to show whether the player is on a block, aiming to improve air mobility in games by providing visual feedback similar to real-life peripheral vision.

## Explanation
The proposal addresses the issue of low air mobility in games by suggesting a visual indicator that mimics the human ability to see feet in peripheral vision. This would help players react more accurately when stepping off blocks, especially with the aid of coyote time and friction mechanics. The maintainer notes that this feature was previously discussed but closed due to unspecified reasons.
