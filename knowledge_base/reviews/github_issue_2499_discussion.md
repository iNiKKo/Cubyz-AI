## Summary

## Explanation
Proposes adding a visual indicator to show whether the player is on a block, aiming to improve air mobility by providing feedback similar to real-life peripheral vision. The proposed indicator would be a small horizontal bar below the crosshair that toggles on or off when the player is on a block. Additionally, when crouching, this line could transform into a topographic view of the floor to help players perceive ledges and walls near their feet. This feature aims to enhance player accuracy in reacting to stepping off blocks with the aid of coyote time and friction mechanics. The maintainer notes that this feature was previously discussed but closed due to unspecified reasons.
