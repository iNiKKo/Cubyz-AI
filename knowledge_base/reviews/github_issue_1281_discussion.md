## Explanation
The proposal to add magnesium to the game has been revised after concerns about its magnetic property potentially negatively impacting younger players. Magnesium is now proposed as a resource for creating brighter torches similar to current white crystals. The physical properties of magnesium include a hardness of 2.5 on the Mohs scale and a density of 1.7 g/cm3 at 20°C. In-game, it behaves similarly to gemstones by providing special effects instead of stats, has low durability and damage, and is light. Magnesium spawns in veins between certain Z coordinates but specific spawn rules are not detailed further. The maintainers decided that magnesium would be more suitable as a resource for creating brighter torches, aligning with the game's progression towards end-game items. This change aims to balance the game's content and ensure accessibility for all age groups.
