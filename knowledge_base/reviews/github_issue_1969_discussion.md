## Explanation
The discussion addresses the issue of low in-air control leading to unintended falls, illustrated with a screenshot showing intended vs actual player movement paths. Proposals include increasing air friction or mobility, implementing 'coyote time' for better jump/fall direction adjustment, and introducing charged jumps where jump height depends on how long the spacebar is held (inspired by Ori and the Blind Forest). There's also consideration of slowing down walking speed upon landing instead of character speed. A prototype was made to adjust landing mechanics but found ineffective at low speeds due to glider physics limitations. The maintainers emphasize testing these possibilities to ensure they improve gameplay without introducing new issues like b-hopping or overly snappy animations.
