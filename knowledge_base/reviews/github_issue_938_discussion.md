## Summary
The issue proposes a radical reimagining of the wildlife food chain in Cubyz, flipping traditional roles. Birds become fish, fish become worms, dogs become spiders, humans become snales, and bugs become mammals.

## Explanation
This proposal introduces a completely new ecological system within Cubyz, where traditional predator-prey relationships are inverted. Specifically, birds become fish that fly through the sky and occasionally dive into water to drink; fish eat worms but in this concept, worms eat fish instead. Dogs transform into cute spiders living in packs, while humans are replaced by snales (likely a typo for 'snails'). Bugs become mammals, with small bugs resembling cats or dogs, and large bugs turning into terrifying beasts that hiss and snarl. The maintainer ultimately scrapped this idea due to changes in the game's lore, indicating that the concept may have been too disruptive or not aligned with the evolving narrative direction of the game.
