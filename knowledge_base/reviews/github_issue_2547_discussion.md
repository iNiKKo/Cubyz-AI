## Summary
Discussion on adding Brambles, a dangerous vegetation item that deals damage and breaks upon contact with entities. Maintainers suggest blending in more with surroundings due to Cubyz's 3D nature and larger block sizes.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around adding Brambles, inspired by Terraria's thorn bushes. Upon contact with an entity, Brambles deal exactly 1 damage to the entity and then break. These can be placed in biomes such as the dead forest, jungle, and taiga as vegetation. The primary concern is making navigation dangerous enough to encourage players to create paths or improve their movement skills without being lethal. Maintainers highlight that Cubyz's 3D environment and larger block sizes make it easier to navigate around obstacles compared to Terraria. They propose that Brambles should blend more with the surroundings, requiring additional harmless vegetation for better integration.
