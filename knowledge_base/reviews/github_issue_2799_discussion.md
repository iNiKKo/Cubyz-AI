## Summary
Discussion on the design and potential enhancements for a silver lamp item that makes ores glow in the dark.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around the design of a passive item called a 'silver lamp' which, when held, causes certain ores to emit light. The primary concern is balancing the effect to ensure it doesn't overwhelm the game environment. There are suggestions for making the lamp's glow more dynamic based on the number of nearby ores or by varying its properties through procedural crafting. Additionally, an alternative behavior involving particle trails leading to nearest ores is proposed.
