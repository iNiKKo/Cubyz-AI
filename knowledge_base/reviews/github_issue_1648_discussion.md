## Explanation
The discussion revolves around adding a unique void biome with floating islands, reverse 'mountains', low gravity, and distinct environmental features. Users suggest mimicking stars and sky effects using specific blocks on the underside of floating islands and the ceiling above the void. The maintainer recommends checking existing biomes like the Abyss and Root biomes for similar ideas and suggests using buoyancy to achieve low gravity effects. However, performance issues in caves were reported, including a freeze where the game stopped rendering any blocks after mining through the ceiling. Users also suggested implementing flipped gravity when mining through the ceiling, with automatic ejection if stuck upside-down for more than 10 seconds. The maintainer cited Terraria's experience with bugs and visual glitches from their gravity flipping potion as reasons to avoid this feature in Cubyz. Ultimately, the issue is closed due to new content guidelines, suggesting that such ideas should first be developed as addons.
