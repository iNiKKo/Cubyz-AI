## Summary
The issue discusses making SBB blocks more visible by rendering them transparently and allowing toggling this feature to avoid interference during the design process.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around improving the visibility of SBB (Structure Building Blocks) when they are buried within structures. The primary suggestion is to render these blocks transparently so that they can be easily seen even when no-clipping inside a structure. Additionally, there is a consideration to add a toggle feature for this transparency to prevent it from interfering with the design process. The maintainer's comment emphasizes the importance of rendering them on top of everything else to ensure visibility.
