## Summary
The discussion revolves around encouraging players to build long transportation systems and permanent bases in Cubyz by making workstations unstackable or difficult to break, thus promoting careful placement and permanent setups.

## Explanation
The issue aims to enhance player engagement by discouraging nomadic playstyles and encouraging the creation of stable structures. The primary focus is on workstations, which are proposed to be either unstackable or require special tools to break, making them less convenient to carry around. This change is intended to encourage players to set up permanent bases for their workstations and other resources. The maintainers discuss various options such as making workstations unbreakable or requiring specific tools with durability penalties to achieve this goal.
