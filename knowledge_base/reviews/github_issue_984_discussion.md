## Explanation
The discussion revolves around encouraging players to build long transportation systems and permanent bases in Cubyz by making workstations unstackable or difficult to break, thus promoting careful placement and permanent setups. The primary focus is on discouraging nomadic playstyles and encouraging the creation of stable structures through game design principles. Specifically, workstations are proposed to be either unstackable or require special tools with durability penalties to break them, making them less convenient to carry around. This change is intended to encourage players to set up permanent bases for their workstations and other resources. The maintainers discuss various options such as making workstations difficult to break by requiring specific tools that also drastically lower durability when used. They emphasize the importance of keeping issues actionable/solvable, ensuring that design principles are implemented in a way that enhances player engagement and user experience.
