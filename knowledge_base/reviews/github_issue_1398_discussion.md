## Summary
Discussion on enhancing pickaxes by encasing them with gold or silver to improve their efficiency on specific stone types.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around the idea of making pickaxes more effective against certain stone types by encasing them with precious metals like gold or silver. The maintainers suggest using tags for limestone and other related blocks instead of specifying each block individually. There is a debate about the amount of metal required (initially proposed as 4 ingots, later reduced to 2) and the power level needed to ensure that the encased pickaxe can one-shot the target stone type. The concept of extensibility in material zones is also mentioned, allowing for customization of the encasing requirements.
