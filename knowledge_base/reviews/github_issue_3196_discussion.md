## Summary
Discussion on adding cobble variants for stones, focusing on sandstone and pyrolite. The team decides to introduce a 'rough' variant instead of cobble for these materials to maintain consistency and provide more building options.

## Explanation
The discussion revolves around the design of stone variants in Cubyz, particularly focusing on sandstone and pyrolite. Initially, there was confusion about whether these stones should have cobble variants or smooth base variants. The maintainer suggests that sandstone and pyrolite are not true cobble but rather have a rough surface, thus recommending the introduction of a 'smooth' version instead. This decision aims to maintain consistency across stone sets and provide more creative building options for players. The team also considers adding a 'rough' variant for other stones like slate, which has distinct, round rocks. The final consensus is to allow crafting between the rough and smooth variants to simplify recipes.
