## Summary
The proposal suggests changing block destruction mechanics to drop shards instead of whole blocks, aiming for more realistic crafting and easier creation of half-block items like stairs and walls. However, previous experiments with similar ideas were deemed cluttery and unnecessary.

## Explanation
The issue proposes altering the game's mechanics so that most blocks (except ores) would drop shards upon destruction instead of full blocks. This change is intended to make the crafting system more realistic by requiring players to use a smaller amount of material for tools and half-block items like stairs and walls. The maintainer, however, has previously experimented with block shards and found them to add clutter to the inventory, lack practical gameplay purpose, and require excessive texturing. They argue that Cubyz's focus on exploration and adventure makes such changes less beneficial compared to games focused on survival.
