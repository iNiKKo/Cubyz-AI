## Summary
The discussion revolves around the complexity of implementing stamina mechanics in Cubyz, including muscle-specific tracking and nutritional effects. The maintainer suggests keeping it simple with a single stamina bar for ease of communication.

## Explanation
The user proposes a detailed model of stamina that includes individual muscle group tracking and different types of muscle fibers, each using varying forms of respiration. This would introduce complexity such as anaerobic respiration depleting hunger more quickly and potentially affecting sprinting differently than running. The maintainer, however, argues against overcomplicating the mechanics, suggesting a single stamina bar is sufficient for player understanding. They also mention that while sleep quality could boost stamina recovery, it should be handled separately. Similarly, simulating muscle growth and nutrient intake would introduce a skilling/levelling system, which might lead to more grinding and complicate biome design.
